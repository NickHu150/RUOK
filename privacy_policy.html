Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Purpose of the Privacy Policy

RUOK? (hereinafter referred to as "the App", "the Service", or "We") is a personal safety system designed specifically for individuals living alone. This policy reflects our commitment to protecting the limited personal data required to provide our "Dead Man's Switch" safety features.

2. Data Collection & Processing

We minimize data collection to only what is strictly necessary for the emergency alert system:

  • Emergency Contact Email: We process the email address of the person you choose to notify in case of an emergency.
  • Personalized Message: Any text you input as your emergency message is stored to be included in the alert email.
  • Check-in Timestamps: We record the time of your last check-in to calculate when the next alert should be scheduled.

3. How We Store Your Data

We prioritize local storage to maximize your privacy control:

  • Local Storage: Your settings and check-in history are stored locally on your device using encrypted data storage. We do not maintain a permanent profile of your activity on our servers.
  • Transient Scheduling: When you perform a check-in, your emergency email and message are temporarily transmitted to our secure backend to schedule the automated alert. This data is only used for the specified delivery purpose.

4. Third-Party Services & SDKs

We use a limited number of sub-processors to ensure reliable email delivery:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Cloudflare Workers Alert Scheduling Encrypted Email ID, Delay Settings
Resend.com Email Delivery Emergency Email, Custom Message

5. Your Rights

Since RUOK? does not require account creation, you have full control over your data directly within the app:

  • Deletion: You can delete all your personal settings and stored data at any time by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App.
  • Modification: You can update your emergency contact and message at any time in the "Settings" section.
